I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the [School of Computer, Data & Information Sciences](https://www.cs.wisc.edu/) at [University of Wisconsin-Madison](https://www.wisc.edu/) where I am fortunate to be advised by [Dr. Sharon Li](https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~sharonli/). My current research focuses on **Agentic Memory for LLM Agents** and **User-Engaged LLM agents**. 

Previously, I earned my M.S. and B.S. degrees in Data Science from [University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)](https://en.ustc.edu.cn/) in 2025 and 2022, respectively, under the supervision of [Dr. Qi Liu](http://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~qiliuql/). My prior research experience spans **perturbation-driven LLM robustness evaluation**, **data mining**, and **cognitive diagnosis for intelligent education**. Throughout my academic journey, I have had the pleasure of collaborating with [Dr. Mengxiao Zhu](http://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~mxzhu/) at USTC, [Dr. Xiang Liu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=rxT-92AAAAAJ) at [Educational Testing Service](https://www.ets.org/), [Dr. Renjun Hu](https://hurenjun.github.io/) at Alibaba Cloud, and [Dr. Yifei Yuan](https://yfyuan01.github.io/) at University of Copenhagen.
