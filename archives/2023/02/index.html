About Me

I am Jiatong Li. I am currently an M.S. student majoring in data science at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. Qi Liu. I got my B.S. degree at USTC in 2022. My research interest includes data mining, deep learning, causal inference, Bayesian learning, and educational measurement. I have abundant research experience in data mining and published one of my research papers in SIGKDD. I have also paper review experience in top conferences and journals such as NeuraIPS, SIGKDD, TKDE, and EDM. In 2021–2022, I used to do internships at Alibaba and iFLYTEK.
